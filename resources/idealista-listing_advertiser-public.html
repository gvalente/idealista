Docta presents this exclusive, completely renovated main residence, located in an elegant regal estate with a valid certificate of aptitude, on the coveted Calle Nàpols, in the heart of the Dreta de l'Eixample. A perfect combination of history, modern design and high-quality finishes in one of the most emblematic areas of Barcelona.

✨ Property Details:
Currently undergoing renovation with completion scheduled for the end of August, this home offers a functional layout, spacious areas and a bright and welcoming atmosphere.

Property Details:

Built area: 80 m²

Main floor in a building with elevator

2 double bedrooms, perfect for ensuring comfort and rest

1 full bathroom + 1 toilet, ideal for everyday life

Independent laundry space, integrated in a practical way

American kitchen with island integrated into a spacious living-dining room, designed to enjoy both everyday life and special moments

2 balconies: One in the master bedroom and another in the living room, with enough space for a small table and chairs, ideal for enjoying the outdoors

Good entry of natural light, which brings warmth to the entire home
? Architectural and renovation characteristics:
This home combines classic charm with modern comfort. Original elements have been preserved that add character and authenticity:
-Catalan vault
-Restored exposed brick walls
-Hydraulic-style design floors
-Comprehensive renovation carried out with high-quality materials, taking care of every detail
-Complete air conditioning through air conditioning and central heating, all electric
-The regal and stately estate, in excellent condition, has an elevator and stands out for its architectural presence.
? Privileged location:
Located in the prestigious Dreta de l'Eixample, this apartment enjoys a lively environment, with shops, restaurants, services and excellent public transport connections. Ideal for those who want to live in the nerve center of Barcelona, with the comfort of a modern home with history.

Note: Brokerage fees are not included in the purchase price.
